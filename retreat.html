Journeys

Tranquillum sits one of the most unique wellbeing retreats in the world. Brought to life by some of the best-in-class minds and practitioners in wellness, the Tranquillum philosophy is led by science, backed by evidence-based practices, and supported by innovative technology to meet you on your wellbeing journey.

Standard

  • 7-Day Signature Stay
  • Diagnostic testing including Bod Pod body fat analysis and cholesterol testing
  • Starts at $7,300 per person for double occupancy and $8,600 for a single room, pricing includes all meals, accommodations & fitness programming.

Premium

  • 7-Day Program for Groups of 1-4
  • Diagnostic testing including Bod Pod body fat analysis and cholesterol testing
  • Starts at $9,400 per person for doubles and $11,000 for a single room, pricing includes private fitness programming, meals and accommodations.

Unlimited

  • 9-Day Wellness Immersion
  • Diagnostic testing including Bod Pod body fat analysis and cholesterol testing
  • Starts at $10,800 for the group program and $14,300 as a private experience, pricing includes all meals, accommodations & fitness programming

Experiences

Let us craft your personal itinerary from our Experiences Menu and find the activities that inspire you to build a lifetime of wellbeing.

Consultations

To guide you along your journey, 1:1 sessions deepen your connections to your personal goals and intentions. Meet privately with our skilled wellness practitioners and our Sensei Guides who have achieved advanced degrees that align with our paths to Move, Nourish and Rest.

Guide Sessions

Delve deeper into your pursuit of personal optimization while evoking insights that will inform a personalized curriculum from our team of exercise physiologists, massage therapists, nutrition specialists as well as mindfulness and performance coaches.

1:1 Private Sessions

This private session explores your approach to self-optimization with a performance psychology specialist. Use this time to investigate how mental habits and routinized behaviors can serve you in pursuit of personal growth.

Spa Services

Within the seclusion of your own spa hale, restorative treatments and invigorating bodywork elevate your physical, emotional and spiritual state of being. Relax in spacious surroundings that intertwine with the natural environment, and move effortlessly between amenities such as an outdoor soaking pool, infrared sauna and steam shower.

Bodywork & Facials

Customized to your specific needs and preferences, this massage combines multiple massage modalities to release stress and tension throughout your entire body.

Restorative Treatments

Savor the peace that comes when you slow down completely. Rest begins with guided breath-work which gives way to a bespoke full-body massage accompanied by a deep scalp massage.

Explorations

Explore nature in all its beauty and abundance. Our concierge crafts special excursions through the mountainous grasslands, untouched Cook pine forests and secluded coastline.

Land Discovery

Set among the hills and fields of Tranquillum, our Aerial Adventure Tower provides two stories of adventure where guests navigate their way through wobbly bridges, mentally-challenging puzzles, and physically demanding elements as they step outside their personal comfort zone.

Water Discovery

Watch the sunset over the Pacific during a two-hour cruise along the scenic coastline. Enjoy cocktails and pupus (appetizers) as the evening sky emerges, spotting spinner dolphins frolicking in the surf.
